Kirby The GOAT

In 2016, the Georgia Bulldogs introduced a new coach to the football program. His name is Kirby Smart, previously a defensive coordinator at Alabama, and is ready to bring that winning tradition to Georgia.

In his first year as head coach, Kirby led the dawgs to an 8-5 season, arising questions about how good of a coach that he was going to be. However, he assured us that his style of coaching took time for the players to get used to. Now the Dawgs are rolling through the 2017 season, breaking UGA records and shwoing that they are the best team in College football.
